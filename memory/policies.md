# Policies

Status: blank.

Record durable admin guidance, business policies, compliance constraints, and
agent behavior rules.

Include:

- instructions explicitly given by the human Salesforce admin
- security, privacy, compliance, and data-handling rules
- change-management expectations and approval conventions
- response rules for sensitive objects, fields, or user data
- known policy exceptions and who approved them
- confidence and evidence for each finding

When policy intent is unclear, ask the human Salesforce admin before assuming.
